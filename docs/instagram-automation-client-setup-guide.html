Instagram Automation Setup and Usage Guide

This document explains what the system does, what your team needs before setup, and how you will use the platform once your Instagram account is connected.

What This System Does

Growth Operations

  • Tracks Instagram performance in one dashboard
  • Organizes content planning and publishing activity
  • Shows comments, DMs, and key follow-up actions

Engagement Automation

  • Listens for comments and incoming intent signals
  • Routes users toward the correct DM or destination link
  • Measures message sends, opens, clicks, and response quality

Pre-Setup Requirements

Access Required

  • Professional Instagram account
  • Business or Creator profile enabled
  • Instagram connected to a Facebook Page
  • Meta / Facebook admin access for approval

Business Inputs Required

  • Brand name and niche
  • Primary goal and posting frequency
  • Sales link, course link, and community link
  • FAQ responses and preferred communication tone

Setup Flow

1

Workspace Setup

We add your brand details, niche, primary goal, posting frequency, team size, and destination links.

2

Instagram Connection

We connect the account through Meta so the system can store the account reference and begin syncing media and available insight data.

3

Intent Routing Setup

We define what should happen when someone leaves a question, inquiry, or collaboration comment.

4

First Sync and Review

We sync the account, verify available data, and review what is automated versus what still needs manual approval.

How Your Team Uses the Dashboard

Overview

Review account health, growth direction, and top priorities for the week.

Content

Review the calendar, see trend signals, and generate hooks and CTAs for upcoming posts.

Inbox

Manage DMs and comment queues, reduce delays, and handle high-priority conversations first.

Analytics

Track message performance, routing outcomes, sync status, and workflow progress.

Settings

Update links, workspace details, Instagram connection, and auto-DM routing logic.

Review Rhythm

Check inbox daily, review analytics weekly, and refine content and routing rules monthly.

What Is Automated and What Still Needs Review

Can Be Automated

  • Comment listening
  • Intent detection
  • Reply routing logic
  • Hook and CTA generation
  • Sync jobs and reporting workflows

Should Stay Human-Reviewed

  • Pricing and sales conversations
  • Partnership or white-label requests
  • Custom or sensitive responses
  • Major changes to brand tone or offers
The platform works best as a growth operations system, not as a spam automation tool. Strong results come from quick replies, clean routing logic, updated links, and regular team review.

Final Client Checklist

  • Instagram account is professional and connected to a Facebook Page
  • Meta admin is available for connection approval
  • Sales, course, and community links are final
  • Team knows who reviews high-intent messages
  • Brand tone and FAQ responses are confirmed
