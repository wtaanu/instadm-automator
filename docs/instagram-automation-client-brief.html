DM Automation in Client Language

Many clients lose opportunities because they reply late, miss pricing questions, or cannot manage all incoming messages. DM automation solves this by organizing the inbox and responding quickly where safe.

  • Detects interest such as price, service inquiry, collaboration, or support
  • Sends approved first replies for simple, repeated questions
  • Routes high-value leads for manual follow-up
  • Keeps response times low and lead quality high
Best practice: use automation for first response and qualification, then hand serious conversations to a person.

Comment Automation in Client Language

Comments are often where interest first appears. A good system helps the client notice and respond to the right comments quickly without posting robotic spam.

  • Detects comments like "price?", "details?", "how to buy?", and "interested"
  • Suggests or sends safe approved replies on owned posts
  • Flags spam, abuse, or low-value comments
  • Supports faster interaction with real prospects
We do not recommend mass commenting on other accounts. That is risky and usually harms brand trust.
